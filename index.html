Project AudioBook

Project AudioBook is a non-profit organisation, which aims to provide free audiobooks of users choice.

Project audiobook caters following things:

  • Based on user’s request we will convert provided document in audio format.
  • You can request as many books as you want to convert in MP3 audio format.
  • There is no upper limit for pages of the document which you provide.

what is AudioBook

Do you like reading books?

Do you feel borring while reading a long book

Do you feel tiring, eye-strain while reading the long documentaries

We got you covered

Because, we provide audiobook of your choice.

You just have to send us files of your choice, may be “.pdf” “.docs” “.txt” and we will convert it into audio format “.mp3” files, so that you can just sit back relax and listen to the AudioBook of your choice.

How to request for AudioBook conversion

when you’re requesting for audiobooks (MP3) conversion, make sure that you are providing following things.

  1. Share your cloud storage (Google Drive, one drive etc) file link in the message box.
  2. give us editing permission, at “projectaudiobook29(at)gmail.com” so that we could fetch your files and send mp3 files back to you.
  3. Please mention your required voice for audiobook conversion. Check the list of synthesizer voices and tell us your required voice in the form below.
  4. if you want Great Books – Audio Books to be featured on our website archives then feel free to reach out.

Notable Features

  • You can request as many audiobook as you want.
  • The audio quality is very dependent on the document which you submit.
  • You can request different voices for different documents.
  • Only one voice will be applicable for one document.
  • The converted audiophile “.mp3” and “.txt” file will be provided back to you.
  • If your prefered language and synthesizer voice is not in our list, then please let us know we will accommodate your needs.
